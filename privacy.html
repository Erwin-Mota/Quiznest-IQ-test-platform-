Introduction

At Testify, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our cognitive assessment platform.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email address (when you provide it for results)
  • Test responses and cognitive assessment data
  • Payment information (processed securely by our partners)
  • Account preferences and settings

Usage Data

  • Test completion times and patterns
  • Platform interaction and navigation
  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and general location data

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our cognitive assessment services
  • Generate personalized test results and insights
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Analyze platform usage to enhance user experience
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

Information Sharing

Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers for payment processing, data analytics, and platform hosting. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users.

Consent

We will only share your information with your explicit consent for specific purposes.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure data storage with AES-256 encryption
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Compliance with data protection regulations

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze platform usage and performance
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations
  • Ensure platform security and functionality

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.
